The [Crypto Commons Association](/solidarity-primitives/crowdstaking/member-projects/crypto-commons-association/) **(CCA)** is a non-profit organization, hosted on Open Collective. It promotes the general development of Crypto Commons infrastructures, through content, research, courses and events. You can pay to attend their events using BREAD!
