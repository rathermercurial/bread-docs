Bread Cooperative is built and sustained by contributors — paid, part-time, and volunteer — working across development, writing, design, marketing, community, and business development. The revolution is a collective effort, and there is always more to do than the core team alone can take on.

If you're considering contributing, the [Contributor Onboarding](/bread-cooperative/contributors/contributor-onboarding/) page is the right place to start. It covers who we're looking for, how the core team works, and how to get involved — including the contributor interest form.

# Pages in this section

- **[Contributor Onboarding](/bread-cooperative/contributors/contributor-onboarding/)** — How to contribute to Bread Cooperative as an individual, what the core team is, and how it operates.
- **[How to be good at EigenLayer](/bread-cooperative/contributors/how-to-eigenlayer/)** — Required reading for contributors working on restaking initiatives like [Gas Killer](/bread-cooperative/sourdough-systems/gas-killer/).

# Other ways to support the project

- **Bake BREAD** — Even a small mint helps. Every BREAD minted backs the [Solidarity Fund](/solidarity-primitives/crowdstaking/) and routes yield to member projects. [app.breadchain.xyz](https://app.breadchain.xyz/)
- **Donate** — Through [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/breadchain-cooperative) (fiat) or [Giveth](https://giveth.io/project/breadchain-cooperative) (crypto, including BREAD).
- **Join the community** — Our [Discord](https://cryptoleftists.xyz/), [Guild](https://era.guild.xyz/breadcoop), and weekly Wednesday community calls.
- **Apply your organization** — See [How to become a member project](/solidarity-primitives/crowdstaking/how-to-become-a-member-project/).
