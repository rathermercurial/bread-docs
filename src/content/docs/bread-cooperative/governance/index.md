title: Bread Cooperative Governance
---

Bread Cooperative is a mutual aid organization and federation of projects exploring the radical potentials of blockchains. How we govern that federation — our shared purpose, guiding principles, eligibility, decision-making, and treasuries — is documented in the two pages below.

This section covers the **off-chain** governance of the cooperative itself: who its members are, how they join and leave, and how the multisigs are run. For the **on-chain** governance of how BREAD yield is distributed across member projects, see [Yield Governance](/solidarity-primitives/crowdstaking/yield-governance/).

# Pages in this section

- **[Bread Constitution](/bread-cooperative/governance/bread-constitution/)** — Our shared animating purpose, guiding principles (Economic Democracy, Mutual Aid, Transparent Governance, Dual Power), and core objectives.
- **[Operational Annex](/bread-cooperative/governance/operational-annex/)** — Practical operating rules: member project eligibility and responsibilities, onboarding, exit and removal processes, the multisigs that hold cooperative funds, and how the Bread Core Team works.
