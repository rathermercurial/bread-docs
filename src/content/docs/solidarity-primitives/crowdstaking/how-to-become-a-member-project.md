We're honored and excited that you're interested in potentially joining as a member project! Here you can find more information about what we expect from projects and what you can expect in the application process. Note that this is the current process and we expect it to change over time as we grow.

We expect projects to be comfortable with being associated with the label **post-capitalist** in that we are critical of how the current system is working and we want to prefigure the infrastructure for what a new system can look like. One that is more democratic and equitable for everyone and not just the few at the top using the tools we have available to us.

While we are utilizing crypto tooling in order to facilitate the system, we do not expect every member project to be very involved in the crypto space. We are interested in both projects that are and ones that are focused on building grassroots movements whether there is crypto involved or not.

Here are our guiding principles which we expect member projects to take to heart.

## BREAD Cooperative Wishlist

These wish lists are meant to help potential future members of BREAD Cooperative know how / if they would be a good fit to joining the cooperative with their own project. While a potential project does not need to be organized exactly according to every point, the more they are aligned with, the more likely they are to be accepted. If you feel that there are particularities about your organization, then feel free to explain when applying as a project in the form.

:::note[How should a potential project be organized?]

• Received no venture capital funding
• Incorporated as a non-profit or cooperative
• Governed as a cooperative / democratically
• Has an existing or planned revenue stream or model
• Organization has at least 3 members
• Have a multisignature wallet where crypto funds are stored
:::

:::note[What should an organizer of a potential member project have?]

• Have used cryptocurrency and defi apps before on Ethereum and Gnosis Chain
• Experience working with progressive grassroots groups
• Have read through the BREAD Cooperative blog
  • https://paragraph.com/@breadcoop
• Joined the BREAD Cooperative Guild
  • https://era.guild.xyz/breadcoop
• Have minted at least 10 BREAD, preferably much more
  • [add new crowdstaking]
• Joined the crypto leftist discord
  • https://cryptoleftists.xyz/
• Checked out our roadmap
  • 🗺️ Roadmap
• Read our constitution
  • 🍞 BREAD Cooperative Constitution
• Have applied through the project form (scroll down)
:::

:::note[What are some of the types of use cases, projects, and organizations we would like to see join BREAD Cooperative?]

This is a collection of projects and use cases shared by current members of BREAD Cooperative that they would like to see be a part of the project. Don't take this as the only kinds of initiatives we will accept.

• An aligned physical community space
• Existing labor unions and cooperatives interested in or already exploring the use of crypto
• Local community organizations interested in exploring the use of crypto
• Crypto based saving circle like a Susu
• Artist and developer collectives
• Tools that make it easier for organizing labor unions and strike action
• Decentralized academic / research institute (p2p ranking of disciplinary knowledge and voting on research for highest tier)
• Decentralized co-op / commune platform (money management through offchain ledgers enforced on chain if needed)
• Decentralized non profit org platform
• Bioinformatic useful proof of work blockchain (DNA sequencing) (https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.03865)
:::

## Steps for joining BREAD Cooperative as a member project

1. Learn about BREAD and what we stand for
2. Use our applications to mint BREAD and vote in a voting cycle
3. Get internal support from your community / team to apply as a member project
4. Fill out the application form
5. Contact someone from BREAD Cooperative that you have done so
6. Expect to wait some time to get a response, we need to go through applications and prioritize
7. If we're interested in having you join then we'll start a conversation
8. We will invite you to join for a discussion with the existing project leads to learn more about you and your project and how it fits into the vision of BREAD Cooperative
9. We will review again to add your organization, **remember that full consensus is required by the project leads at the moment**
10. If accepted we will ask for some assets to add your project as an option on the governance page
11. Fill out your member project's card with the information asked
12. Make sure you have 3000 BREAD in your treasury
13. Adding new members can only happen at the start of a new voting cycle so we will add you at the start of the next one once all other steps are completed

## Expectations of a member project

Once accepted, all member projects are expected to:

- **Nominate a project representative that will:**
  - be a part of the multisig to sign transactions
  - join monthly meetings
  - give updates on their project
  - relay information from the meeting to the rest of their team
- **Add a link / logo to the BREAD homepage on their website**
- **Share with their community that they have joined BREAD Cooperative**
- **Hold a minimum of 3,000 BREAD in their treasuries**
- **Actively recruit their community to mint BREAD**
- **Prioritize the use of BREAD within their community however that looks in their context**
- **Support other member projects for increasing network effects**
