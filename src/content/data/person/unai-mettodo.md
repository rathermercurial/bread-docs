## About

Community steward hosting online events, informing the community about Breadchain's achievements, and encouraging the cryptoleftists community members to actively support each other and contribute to shared projects.

## Strategy

- Increase surface area and interpersonal trust over anything else
- Work with people who are already in Crypto Leftists Discord over bringing new people in
- Get people to know Breadchain more and smoothen the processes for their financial and non-financial support over initiating new collective projects
- Identify and work with existing IRL communities that are aligned over trying to make our own

## Accountabilities

- Designing, promoting and facilitating events in Crypto Leftists Discord server
- Creating and sharing artifacts out of the events
- Updating Notion calendar with events
- Connecting with community members and aligned communities to organise events
- Drafting and sending a monthly newsletter
- Managing and promoting Jokeraces for the community to select temp check topics
- Collaborating with devs to create a smooth contribution pipeline for Breadchain
- Designing and testing IRL initiatives to connect Breadchain and IRL communities
