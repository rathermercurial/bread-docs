# Wholesale Organic Produce

## Overview

Local Harvest Food Cooperative offers wholesale organic produce to restaurants, institutions, schools, and community food programs. All products are sourced from regional farmers within 100 miles of our cooperative.

## Available Products

### Fresh Produce
- **Seasonal Vegetables**: Tomatoes, leafy greens, root vegetables, squash
- **Fresh Herbs**: Basil, cilantro, parsley, and specialty herbs
- **Fruits**: Berries, apples, stone fruits (seasonal availability)
- **Specialty Items**: Microgreens, edible flowers, heirloom varieties

### Farm Products
- Fresh eggs from pasture-raised chickens
- Artisan bread from local bakeries
- Honey and maple syrup
- Preserves and fermented foods

## Sourcing

We work with 15+ regional farmers who practice:
- **Organic methods** (certified and uncertified)
- **Regenerative agriculture**
- **Fair labor practices**
- **Biodiverse farming**

## Pricing

Wholesale pricing is:
- **20-30% below retail** for standard orders
- **Volume discounts** for large institutions
- **Seasonal variation** based on availability
- **Fair prices** ensuring farmer sustainability

## Ordering

### Minimum Orders
- Restaurants: $100 minimum
- Institutions/Schools: $250 minimum
- Community Programs: Flexible minimums

### Delivery
- **Local Area**: 2x weekly delivery included
- **Extended Range**: Weekly delivery available
- **Self-Pickup**: Available at cooperative location

### Ordering Process
1. Request current availability list
2. Place order (48 hours advance for standard, 1 week for large orders)
3. Receive delivery or arrange pickup
4. Invoice due net-30 for established accounts

## Quality Guarantee

- Fresh, high-quality products
- 100% satisfaction guarantee
- Imperfect produce available at reduced cost
- Transparent sourcing information

## Values

As a cooperative, we're committed to:
- **Fair Prices**: For both farmers and buyers
- **Local Economy**: Building regional food systems
- **Sustainability**: Environmental and social
- **Transparency**: Know your farmers and sourcing

## Special Programs

### Community Food Programs
We offer discounted rates for:
- Food pantries and hunger relief
- Community fridges and mutual aid
- Sliding-scale community meals
- School lunch programs

### Cooperative Bulk Buying
Other cooperatives and buyer clubs can access wholesale pricing for member food access programs.

## Current Availability

Contact us for weekly availability list and pricing:
- Spring/Summer: High volume, diverse selection
- Fall: Root vegetables, storage crops, preserves
- Winter: Storage crops, greenhouse greens, preserved items
