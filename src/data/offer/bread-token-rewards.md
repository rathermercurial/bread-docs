# BREAD Token Rewards & Benefits

## What is BREAD?

BREAD is the native token of the Breadchain solidarity economy network. It represents participation in cooperative governance and provides access to network benefits and rewards.

## How to Earn BREAD

### Angel Minting (Crowdstaking)
- Stake ETH or stablecoins in yield-generating protocols
- Yield is used to mint BREAD tokens
- Tokens distributed to:
  - Angel Minters (stakers)
  - Member Projects (cooperatives)
  - Contributors (developers, organizers)

### Contributions
- Develop code and documentation
- Organize community events
- Participate in governance
- Support member projects

### Member Project Rewards
- Cooperatives receive BREAD allocations
- Based on participation and needs
- Democratic distribution to members

## Benefits & Utility

### Governance Rights
- Vote on protocol parameters
- Participate in cooperative decision-making
- Influence BREAD distribution
- Shape network direction

### Economic Benefits
- **Discounts**: Member project goods/services
- **Priority Access**: Limited offerings and opportunities
- **Rewards**: Additional BREAD through participation
- **Yield Share**: Portion of protocol yield

### Network Access
- Connect with cooperative ecosystem
- Mutual aid coordination
- Resource sharing
- Collaborative opportunities

## Current Offerings

### For Angel Minters
- Earn BREAD through crowdstaking
- Support solidarity economy projects
- Receive yield share from protocols
- Participate in governance

### For Member Projects
- Receive BREAD allocations
- Access to network resources
- Technical support and infrastructure
- Cooperative development funds

### For Contributors
- Compensation for development work
- Recognition and rewards
- Governance participation
- Community support

## Token Economics

- **Supply**: Minted through crowdstaking yield
- **Distribution**:
  - 40% Angel Minters
  - 40% Member Projects
  - 20% Contributors
- **Governance**: One-token-one-vote on specific proposals
- **Liquidity**: Available on decentralized exchanges

## How to Participate

### Become an Angel Minter
1. Review crowdstaking documentation
2. Connect wallet to Breadchain app
3. Stake ETH or stablecoins
4. Receive BREAD tokens automatically

### Join as Member Project
1. Apply through Breadchain governance
2. Community review and vote
3. Onboarding and support
4. Begin receiving allocations

### Contribute to Breadchain
1. Join Discord and introduce yourself
2. Find areas aligned with your skills
3. Submit contributions
4. Receive BREAD rewards

## Values & Principles
