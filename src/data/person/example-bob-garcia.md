# Bob Garcia

## About

Bob is a graphic designer and visual communicator who helps cooperatives develop their brand identity and communication materials. He believes in making design accessible to grassroots organizations.

## Background

After a decade working in corporate design agencies, Bob transitioned to working exclusively with cooperatives, non-profits, and mutual aid organizations. He's passionate about visual storytelling for social movements.

## Contributions to Breadchain

- Designed the Bread Design System
- Created visual assets for documentation
- Teaches design workshops for cooperative members
- Maintains the Breadchain brand guidelines

## Skills

- Brand identity and visual design
- Typography and layout design
- Design systems and style guides
- Workshop facilitation
