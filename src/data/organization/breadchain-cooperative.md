# Breadchain Cooperative

## About

Breadchain is a solidarity economy platform that connects cooperative projects, mutual aid networks, and regenerative finance mechanisms. We're building infrastructure for post-capitalist economic relations.

## Mission

Our mission is to create sustainable funding mechanisms for solidarity economy projects through innovative use of blockchain technology, while maintaining cooperative governance and democratic decision-making.

## What We Do

- **BREAD Token**: A regenerative currency that rewards cooperative projects and mutual aid work
- **Angel Minting**: Community-driven funding mechanism for solidarity economy initiatives
- **Cooperative Support**: Technical infrastructure, documentation, and organizing resources
- **Network Building**: Connecting cooperatives, mutual aid groups, and solidarity projects

## Projects

- Breadchain Documentation Platform
- BREAD Token economic system
- Crowdstaking infrastructure
- Cooperative governance tools

## Members

Our cooperative includes developers, designers, organizers, bakers, farmers, and community members working together to build solidarity economy infrastructure.
